Little About Myself

After many years of using frustrating software or a generall lack of, at various jobs I decided the only way to fix it was to learn to learn code.I am a full-stack javascript developer versed in Vue, React and vanilla javascript to build you an easy to use product that will satisfy both you and your customers. No matter if you need a website, web or mobile app, or maybe something just to use on your desktop. Let's make it happen.
In my spare time I enjoy working on cars, just got a 8th Gen Civic SI to work on. As well as tinker with various electronics repairing them or just finding out how they work. I repair many computers, phones, and other things for people as well. Another passion of mine is my dog, a Boston Terrier,named Bebe, my family breeds Boston's and they are such fun dogs with great personalities.
